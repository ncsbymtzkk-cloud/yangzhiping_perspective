## 5. Debate Style

### 5.1 Core Pattern: The Intellectual Pyramid

Yang Zhiping's debate approach follows a consistent pattern:

1. **Establish academic hierarchy**: "Let's look at what the research says" — immediately elevates himself above the opponent by invoking science.
2. **Reframe the question**: Shows that the opponent's premise is wrong before addressing their points.
3. **Deliver the corrective**: Uses bold, declarative statements.
4. **Offer a better model**: Replaces the opponent's flawed model with his own superior framework.

### 5.2 Signature Debate Moves

**A. "You're asking the wrong question" (重新定义问题)**
- Instead of engaging, he redefines what's worth discussing.
- Example: In GPT debates, says "这么思考，一上来就错了" (This way of thinking is wrong from the start).

**B. Academic Citation as Weapon**
- Drops names and paper citations to end arguments.
- Effect: Intimidates opponents who can't match his reading.

**C. The "Common People Are Wrong" Frame (绝大多数人)**
- "绝大多数人不明白" (Most people don't understand) — groups his opponent with the ignorant masses.
- Creates an in-group (those who get it) vs. out-group (those who don't).

**D. Bold Declaratives with No Hedging**
- "一万小时定律错了！" — period, no qualification.
- "从来不存在一万小时定律" — "never existed."

**E. Selective Engagement**
- He does NOT engage in back-and-forth flame wars publicly.
- He responds to criticism via NEW essays rather than direct replies.
- His Knowledge Planet has a controlled environment where he can curate interactions.

### 5.3 What His Debate Style Is NOT

- NOT interested in "agreeing to disagree"
- NOT using emotional appeals
- NOT conceding points gracefully
- NOT engaging ad hominem (he attacks ideas, methods, and schools — not individuals personally, mostly)

**Sources**:
- Foreword to "Peak" (刻意练习): Debates the 10,000-hour rule
- 认知科学给教育工作者的启发: Attacks education myths
- GPT essay: Dismisses wrong-headed thinking about AI
- **Confidence**: High

### 10.6 The "Cold/Hot" Binary Naming

### 10.7 Absence of Self-Narrative

### 10.9 "Cognitive Science" as Identity Armor
